Features

Everything you need in
an AI companion

Built for natural conversation. Designed for privacy. Open for everyone.

Hands-Free Voice Chat

Always-on speech recognition. Talk naturally and your message auto-sends after a brief pause. Live captions for both sides.

3D Anime Avatar

Fully animated VRM character with lip sync, idle animations, and expressions. Powered by Three.js and Amica.

Vision

Front camera feeds to the AI so it can see what you see. No preview shown by default. Privacy-first design.

Persistent Memory

AI extracts and remembers key details across conversations. Builds context over time for personal interactions.

Multi-Provider LLM

Choose your AI brain: Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, Ollama, OpenRouter, xAI, or Together AI. Switch anytime.

Premium TTS

ElevenLabs, OpenAI TTS, or native iOS voices. Natural and expressive text-to-speech that brings your companion to life.
