Features

Everything built in

Natural conversation. Privacy by default. Your choice of AI.

Hands-Free Voice

Always-on speech recognition. Talk naturally, auto-sends after a pause. Live captions for both sides.

3D Anime Avatar

Animated VRM character with lip sync, idle animations, and expressions. Three.js + Amica engine.

Vision

Front camera feeds to AI. No preview by default. She sees what you see, privacy-first.

Persistent Memory

Extracts and remembers key details across conversations. Context that grows over time.

Multi-Provider LLM

Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, Ollama, OpenRouter, xAI, Together AI. Switch anytime in settings.

Premium TTS

ElevenLabs, OpenAI TTS, or native iOS. Expressive text-to-speech that brings her to life.
