Features

🗣

Hands-Free Voice Chat

Always-on speech recognition. Just talk naturally and your message auto-sends after a brief pause. Live captions show what you and the AI are saying.

🧑‍🎤

3D Anime Avatar

A fully animated VRM character with lip sync, idle animations, and expressions. Powered by Three.js and Amica's open-source avatar engine.

👁

Vision

Front camera feeds to the AI so it can see what you see. No preview shown by default for a clean, privacy-first experience.

🧠

Persistent Memory

The AI extracts and remembers key details from your conversations. It builds context over time so interactions feel personal.

🔄

Multi-Provider LLM

Choose your AI brain: Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, Ollama, OpenRouter, xAI, or Together AI. Switch anytime in settings.

🔊

Premium TTS

Hear your companion speak with ElevenLabs, OpenAI TTS, or native iOS voices. Natural and expressive text-to-speech.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Scowld is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to use cloud AI providers.

Data Collection

Scowld does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We do not have servers. The app runs entirely on your device.

Third-Party Services

When you configure an AI provider (Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, etc.), your messages are sent to that provider's API for processing. This is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini: ai.google.dev/terms
  • OpenAI: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Anthropic (Claude): anthropic.com/privacy
  • ElevenLabs: elevenlabs.io/privacy

Camera & Microphone

Camera and microphone data is processed on-device for speech recognition (Apple Speech framework) and sent to your configured AI provider only when you send a message. No audio or video is stored or transmitted elsewhere.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device. They are never transmitted to us or any third party other than the respective AI provider.

Local Storage

Chat history and memories are stored locally on your device using CoreData. You can clear all data at any time from Settings.
