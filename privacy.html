Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Scowld is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to use cloud AI providers.

Data Collection

Scowld does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We do not have servers. The app runs entirely on your device.

Third-Party Services

When you configure an AI provider, your messages are sent to that provider's API:

  • Google Gemini: ai.google.dev/terms
  • OpenAI: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Anthropic (Claude): anthropic.com/privacy
  • ElevenLabs: elevenlabs.io/privacy

Camera & Microphone

Camera and microphone data is processed on-device for speech recognition and sent to your configured AI provider only when you send a message.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain. They are never transmitted to us.

Local Storage

Chat history and memories are stored locally using CoreData. You can clear all data from Settings.
